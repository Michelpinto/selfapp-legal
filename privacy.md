# Privacy Policy for Self

**Last Updated: January 28, 2026**

## Introduction

Self ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Data
- **Anonymous Account**: We create an anonymous user account using Supabase authentication. No email, phone number, or personal identifiers are required.
- **User-Generated Content**: Identities, daily actions, action completions, journal entries, and reflections you create within the app.
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use the app, stored locally on your device.

### Automatically Collected Data
- **Device Information**: We do not collect device-specific information.
- **Analytics**: Currently, we do not use analytics services.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the app functionality
- Sync your data across devices (if you reinstall the app)
- Enable cloud backup of your personal data
- Process subscription payments (via Apple's App Store)

## Data Storage and Security

- **Cloud Storage**: Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a hosted PostgreSQL database with encryption in transit and at rest.
- **Local Storage**: Data is also stored locally on your device using SwiftData.
- **Anonymous Authentication**: You are identified only by a randomly generated UUID. We cannot link your data to your real identity.

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal data
- Share your data with third parties for advertising
- Use your data for marketing purposes

We MAY share data with:
- **Supabase**: Our cloud database provider (see [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy))
- **Apple**: For subscription management through the App Store
- **RevenueCat**: For subscription management and analytics (see [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy))

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data associated with your account
- **Export**: Download your data in JSON format (Pro feature)
- **Delete**: Remove all your data from our servers by resetting your cloud session in the app settings
- **Opt-Out**: Stop using the app at any time

## Data Retention

- Your data is retained as long as you use the app
- If you reset your cloud session, all data is permanently deleted from our servers
- Local data remains on your device until you delete the app

## Children's Privacy

Self is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Subscriptions

- Subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store
- We do not store your payment information
- Subscription management follows Apple's standard practices
- See [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
